ABOUT TILSYN

Digital Safety for the Next Generation

In the modern era, children are being raised with unprecedented access to the internet and online platforms. Digital connectivity has become an integral part of every child's life, offering a wealth of educational resources and opportunities. But with these benefits come significant risks.

THE CHALLENGE WE ADDRESS

Children today face risks such as cyberbullying, online predators, exposure to inappropriate content, and harmful ideologies. While parents should be a child's first line of defense, not all have the time or technical expertise to effectively monitor their children's digital activities.

This situation places immense pressure on parents, who often feel overwhelmed by the volume of digital content their child consumes and the rapid pace of technological change. Many parents lack the digital literacy necessary to guide their children, creating a gap in their ability to understand and mitigate online risks.

The demands of modern work life frequently leave parents with limited time and energy to engage in meaningful conversations about online safety. This can lead to a sense of helplessness as parents struggle to keep up with their children's digital activities. The constant evolution of online platforms and apps presents an ongoing challenge, requiring parents to continuously learn and adapt.

OUR SOLUTION

EMPOWERING PARENTS

Tilsyn provides parents with intuitive tools to monitor their children's online activities without requiring technical expertise.

BALANCED MONITORING

We strike the perfect balance between safety and privacy, respecting children's autonomy while ensuring their protection.

FOSTERING DIALOGUE

Our tools are designed to encourage open conversations about digital safety between parents and children.
